Getting started
MyCommercePartner is a listing optimization platform built for eBay sellers. You paste in your current title, description, condition, pricing, and item specifics — and we generate a stronger version with a cleaner title, better bullet points, improved description, and a prioritized action plan to improve search visibility and conversion.
You can explore the live demo on the homepage without an account. To save your work and run real optimizations, you'll need to create a free account. Signing up gives you 2 free optimization credits to use before choosing a plan.
The more detail you provide, the better the output. At minimum you need a product title, category, and condition. Adding item specifics, a current description, your price, and shipping details gives the optimizer more to work with and produces stronger recommendations.
MyCommercePartner works across all eBay categories. It has enhanced support for Electronics, Cell Phones, Laptops, Auto Parts, and Fashion — with category-specific title logic, bullet templates, and specifics recommendations. You can also enter a custom category for anything outside the preset list.
Optimization
Each optimization run produces: a rewritten title (within eBay's 80-character limit), recommended item specifics, bullet selling points, an optimized description, a list of next best moves, and a scoring breakdown across SEO, conversion, and completeness.
The optimizer restructures your title around high-priority search terms for your category, places the most important keywords toward the front, removes filler words, and formats everything to fit within eBay's 80-character limit. The goal is a title that both ranks well in search and reads naturally to buyers.
Yes. You can upload a product image directly in the workspace. It will appear alongside your optimized description output, making it easier to review the full listing package before applying it to eBay.
SEO score measures how well your listing is positioned to appear in eBay search results — keyword placement, title structure, and specifics completeness. Conversion score measures how likely a buyer who finds your listing is to make a purchase — based on trust signals, condition clarity, and description quality. Overall score is a combined view of listing health across both dimensions.
Most optimizations complete in under 10 seconds. A typical listing review and rewrite cycle — from pasting your details to having publish-ready copy — takes around 3 minutes.
