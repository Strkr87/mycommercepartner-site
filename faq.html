FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about listing optimization, audits, pricing, and how MyCommercePartner helps eBay sellers improve conversion.

What does MyCommercePartner do?

MyCommercePartner helps eBay sellers improve listing titles, buyer-facing copy, item specifics, and overall listing presentation so products are easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to buy.

Who is it best for?

It is best for resellers, repeat sellers, and inventory-heavy stores that want stronger listings without rewriting everything from scratch.

What are the 2 free optimizations?

New users can try the workflow with 2 free listing optimizations after creating an account, so they can test the value before moving into a paid option.

What is the $49 Listing Conversion Audit?

The audit is a lower-friction paid offer where 10 listings are reviewed for weak titles, missing specifics, trust issues, and conversion bottlenecks. You receive prioritized recommendations you can act on quickly.

Do you offer monthly plans?

Yes. MyCommercePartner offers monthly credit-based plans for sellers who want to improve listings consistently over time.

What is the difference between a basic and enhanced listing?

A basic listing often relies on plain text and weak buyer communication. An enhanced listing uses better keyword coverage, benefit-focused bullets, stronger structure, and a cleaner product presentation.

Can you help beyond software?

Yes. In addition to the optimizer and audit, MyCommercePartner can support rewrite packages, listing cleanup, and store tune-up style help.

How do I get started?

The easiest path is to create an account and use the 2 free optimizations, or start with the $49 audit if you want a faster paid entry point.
